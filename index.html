    Who is Billy Bob Jackson?

  • Has an infinite supply of dad jokes and puns, will laugh every time.
  • Strong daddy instincts means he dads just about everybody.
  • Grew up in Piscari right as crime became a big thing.
  • Moved away from Piscari when he hit 18 and came to Uberrime.
  • Did manual labor for several years and then a desk job for several more.
  • Recently fired and willing to do anything, moral or not, for his family.

    Family

  • Lina

    Lina is Billy Bob's wife. They've known each other for 18 years and got married 15 years ago. She's acutely aware that everybody she passes by has their own story and makes it her life's goal to leave at least one good memory in each person she meets. That's why Billy Bob and Lina first started talking, actually.

  • Emilia

    Emilia is their 4 y/o daughter. She's got brown curls and wide hazel eyes that are just perfect for the puppy-eye look. She likes watching movies with her parents, toys, and making flower crowns for princesses (everybody is a princess, including daddy).

  • Hannah

    A romanticist at heart and Billy Bob's mom. She was born 61 years ago. She left a comfortable life in Okeanos to be together with his father, Kahili. While their relationship is almost non-existent now, Billy Bob still appreciates her and wishes the best for her.

  • Kahili

    His work is uncertain, but he must be getting money from somewhere, because he sure spends a lot of it on gambling and alcohol. Apparently once upon a time there was something loveable about him, but Billy Bob has never seen it. He refers to his father by 'Kahili' and their relationship is rocky at best.
